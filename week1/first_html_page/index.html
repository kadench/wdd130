For those wondering, I have cerebral palsy. I had a brain hemorrhage at birth. This was caused by many complications between my twin and I. Basically, in the womb, we were sharing blood. This is known as twin-to-twin transfusion. so when we were born, I had most of our blood and my brother basically had none and was severely underdeveloped. All the blood that I had in my body rushed to my brain, giving me a grade 4 brain hemorrhage. The doctors believed this would leave me unable to talk, walk or move, but by some miracle, all the holes formed by the hemorrhage were gone by the time we went home. My twin brother had died when he was born, but the doctors were able to bring him back. We're both alive and well now.
